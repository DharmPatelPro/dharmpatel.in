About Dharm Patel

Namaste, I am Dharm Patel. I am a Robotics & Automation Engineer passionate about building intelligent systems. My expertise lies in designing precise control systems, programming embedded hardware (STM32, IoT), and creating robust mechanical designs using Onshape.

I bring a proven track record of high-performance engineering, demonstrated by representing India on the international stage at ABU Robocon 2025. Beyond technical development, I possess strong leadership capabilities, having successfully trained and directed hundreds of students in robotics workshops. I am eager to apply these skills to contribute to your organization's vision.

My Vision

My vision is to design robotics and automation systems based on the RRR principle—Reliability, Redundancy, and Robustness. I focus on building systems that are structurally sound, algorithmically stable, and resilient to uncertainties in environment.
