About me

Namaste, I am Dharm Patel. I am a Robotics & Automation Engineer passionate about building intelligent systems. My expertise lies in designing precise control systems, programming embedded hardware,robotics and creating robust designs.

I bring a proven track record of high-performance engineering, demonstrated by representing India on the international stage at ABU Robocon 2025. Beyond technical development, I possess strong leadership capabilities, having successfully trained and directed hundreds of students in robotics workshops.

My Vision

My vision is to design robotics and automation systems based on the RRR principle—Reliability, Redundancy, and Robustness. I focus on building systems that are structurally sound, algorithmically stable, and resilient to uncertainties in environment.
